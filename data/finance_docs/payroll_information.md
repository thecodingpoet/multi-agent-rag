# Payroll Information

## Pay Schedule

**Bi-weekly Fridays**, funds by 9 AM, pay stubs 24hrs before
**Full calendar**: https://payroll.company.com/schedule
**Holiday**: Payment on previous business day

## Direct Deposit

**Setup**: https://hr.company.com/myinfo by Tuesday before payday
**Required**: Routing number, account number, type, voided check
**Split**: Up to 3 accounts
**First check**: Paper if direct deposit not ready

## Pay Components

**Gross**: Base salary (รท26), overtime (1.5x), bonuses, commissions
**Pre-Tax Deductions**: 401(k), health insurance, HSA/FSA, commuter
**Taxes**: Federal, state, Social Security (6.2%), Medicare (1.45% + 0.9% over $200k)
**Post-Tax**: Roth 401(k), life insurance, charitable giving

## Time Reporting

**Exempt**: No tracking, full salary
**Non-Exempt**: Submit weekly by Monday 9 AM at https://time.company.com, OT pre-approved (1.5x), 30min unpaid meal break (6+ hr shifts)

## Tax & Year-End

**W-4**: Update anytime via portal, use IRS calculator
**W-2**: Jan 31 via https://my.adp.com
**1095-C**: Mar 31 (health coverage proof)

## Bonuses

**Performance**: March payout, 22% tax rate
**Commissions**: Monthly, prior month's deals
**Sign-On**: Per offer letter, repay if leave early

## Corrections & Leave

**Errors**: Report to payroll@company.com, 2-day fix, off-cycle if $100+
**Paid Leave**: Vacation, sick, holidays, bereavement (5 days)
**Unpaid**: FMLA, personal leave
**Disability**: Insurance carrier pays percentage

## Final Pay & Verification

**Resignation**: Next payday, includes vacation (not sick time)
**Termination**: Immediate or per state law
**Verification**: The Work Number (Code: 12345, 1-800-367-5690)
