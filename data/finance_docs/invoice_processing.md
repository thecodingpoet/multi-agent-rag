# Invoice Processing and Accounts Payable

## Invoice Submission

**Methods**: ap@company.com (preferred), mail, https://vendor.company.com/invoices

**Required Info**: Vendor name/address, invoice number/date, payment terms, PO number ($500+), itemized description, quantities, total, payment instructions

**Missing info = rejected invoice**

## Payment

**Terms**: Net 30, 2% discount if Net 10
**Processing**: Thursdays weekly, ACH preferred
**Timeline**: Submit Mon → Approved by Fri → Paid in 30 days

## Approval

**Auto-Approved**: Invoice matches PO (within 5%)
**Manual**: Manager approval within 5 days if no PO match or over $500 without PO

## Purchase Orders

**Required**: All $500+ purchases, recurring services, contracts, equipment, software
**Format**: PO-2025-XXXXX
**Without PO**: Rejected if over $500

## Disputes & Issues

**Common**: Price/quantity mismatch, wrong items, duplicates
**Resolution**: Email ap@company.com with invoice/PO numbers, 3-day review

## EDI & ACH

**EDI**: High-volume vendors, auto-transmission, faster processing (edi@company.com)
**ACH**: 1-2 day payment vs. 5-7 for checks, setup at https://vendor.company.com/ach, active in 2 weeks

## 1099 Reporting

**When**: $600+ annually for services to unincorporated businesses
**W-9**: Required before payment
**Forms**: Mailed by Jan 31 (tax@company.com for questions)

## Status Tracking

**Portal**: https://vendor.company.com (upload, track, view history, update banking)

**Statuses**: Received (processing) → Pending Approval (contact if 5+ days) → Approved (next run) → Paid (1-2 days)

## Payment Delays

Missing PO, PO mismatch, missing W-9, wrong address, manager on vacation, duplicate

## International & Credits

**International**: SWIFT/IBAN required, USD or local currency, company pays outgoing wire fees
**Credit Memos**: Reference original invoice, 10-day processing, applied or refunded

## Inquiries & Best Practices

**Before contacting**: Check portal, verify 30 days passed, confirm PO included
**Contact**: ap@company.com | ext. 5630 | Mon-Fri 8 AM-5 PM EST

**Best Practices**: Include PO, consistent numbering, set up ACH, keep W-9 current, use portal
