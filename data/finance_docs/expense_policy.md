# Expense Reimbursement Policy

## Eligible Expenses

**Travel**: Airfare (economy), hotels (standard room), ground transport, meals, baggage fees
**Business Meals**: Client meetings, team meals during extended hours
**Supplies**: Books, publications, office supplies, professional memberships
**Development**: Conference fees, training, certifications

## Spending Limits

| Category | Daily/Per Item Limit | Approval Required |
|----------|---------------------|-------------------|
| Meals (breakfast) | $20 | None |
| Meals (lunch) | $25 | None |
| Meals (dinner) | $50 | None |
| Hotel (standard cities) | $200/night | Over $250 |
| Hotel (high-cost cities) | $300/night | Over $350 |
| Rental car | Economy/mid-size | Premium class |
| Client entertainment | $100/person | Over $200/person |

## Submission Requirements

### Timing
- Submit within **30 days** of expense date
- Late submissions may be denied
- Month-end expenses due by 5th of following month

### Documentation
**Required for all expenses:**
- Original itemized receipt (photo or PDF)
- Business purpose and attendees (for meals)
- Dates and location

**Credit card statements not accepted**

### Submission Process
1. Log into expense portal: https://expenses.company.com
2. Create new expense report
3. Upload receipts and categorize expenses
4. Add business justification
5. Submit for manager approval

## Reimbursement Timeline

- **Manager approval**: 3 business days
- **Finance review**: 2 business days
- **Payment processing**: 5-7 business days after approval
- **Total**: Approximately 10-12 business days

Payment via direct deposit to bank account on file.

## Non-Reimbursable

❌ Personal entertainment, alcohol (except client), fines, grooming, unauthorized upgrades, minibar, pet care, lost items

## Corporate Card

- Business use only, reconcile by 20th monthly
- Violations: Warning → 30-day suspension → Revocation

## Additional Policies

**Travel Advances**: $2,000 max, 2 weeks prior request, reconcile within 7 days
**Mileage**: $0.67/mile (IRS rate), requires destination and purpose
**Approvals**: Director ($500+), VP ($2,000+), CFO ($5,000+)
