# Travel Reimbursement Guidelines

## Pre-Travel Approval

**Domestic**: Manager approval, book 14 days advance via https://travel.company.com
**International**: VP approval, book 30 days advance, complete risk assessment

## Booking

**Flights**: Economy (<6hrs), Premium economy (6+hrs with approval), United/Delta/American preferred
**Hotels**: Corporate rates via platform, standard room only
**Ground**: Ride-share preferred, taxi, shuttle, public transit, rental (economy/mid-size only)
**Rental Cars**: Decline insurance, return full tank, no GPS/child seats

## Meals & Per Diem

**Domestic Per Diem**: $75/day (breakfast $15, lunch $20, dinner $40), no receipts needed
**International**: GSA rates by location
**Actual Expenses**: Receipts required, manager approval to exceed per diem
**Tips**: Up to 20%, alcohol only for client entertainment

## Travel Companions & Disruptions

**Personal Travel**: Not reimbursed, business portion only if combined
**Delays**: Meals (3+ hour delays) and hotels reimbursed
**Insurance**: Auto-included via company platform
**Loyalty Programs**: Keep miles/points, don't choose expensive options for points

## International

**Currency**: Use corporate card, convert at bank not airport
**Phone**: $15/day max or local SIM
**Vaccinations**: Required (fully covered), recommended (with approval)
**Safety**: Register with security, download travel app, check advisories

## Expense Reporting

**Required**: Flight confirmations, hotel folio, receipts (itemized)
**Deadline**: 30 days after trip
**Missing Receipts**: Under $25 (explanation), over $25 (not reimbursed)
