# Purchasing and Procurement Guidelines

## Purchase Authority Levels

| Amount | Approval Required | Process |
|--------|------------------|---------|
| Under $500 | Department budget owner | Direct purchase |
| $500 - $2,500 | Manager | Purchase request form |
| $2,500 - $10,000 | Director | Procurement team |
| $10,000 - $50,000 | VP + Procurement | Vendor quotes (3 minimum) |
| Over $50,000 | CFO + Procurement | Formal RFP process |

## Purchasing Methods

**Corporate Card**: Under $500, receipts required, reconcile by 20th
**Purchase Orders**: Over $500, submit at https://procurement.company.com, issued in 3-5 days
**Procurement Team**: Over $2,500, software, hardware, subscriptions (ext. 5620)

## Vendors

**Preferred**: Office Depot/Staples (supplies), Dell/Apple/CDW (tech), Microsoft/Adobe (software), AWS/GCP/Azure (cloud)
**New Vendors**: Submit info form, W-9, insurance cert, 5-10 day assessment

## Software & Hardware

**Software**: Check existing licenses, get IT approval, annual subscription review
**Hardware**: IT provides standard equipment, ergonomic up to $500 with manager approval

## Contracts & Emergency Purchases

**Annual Contracts**: 60-day pre-renewal review, no auto-renewal without confirmation
**Subscriptions**: Quarterly reviews, cancel unused (subscriptions@company.com)
**Emergency**: Verbal approval, purchase, submit within 24hrs with justification

## Competitive Bidding

Required for $10k+ purchases, new vendors, $25k+ renewals. Get 3 quotes minimum.

## Returns & Disputes

Contact vendor immediately, document issues, notify procurement. Billing errors to ap@company.com.

## Invoicing & Budget

**Invoices**: Email ap@company.com with PO number, Net 30 terms, processed Thursdays
**Budget**: Monthly tracking, executive approval for over-budget, transfers via budgets@company.com
**Records**: 7-year retention, quarterly audits
