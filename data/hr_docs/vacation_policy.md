# Vacation Policy

## Annual Vacation Entitlement

- All full-time employees have a minimum of 15 working days of vacation per calendar year.
- After 3 years of tenure, the benefit increases to 20 working days.
- After 5 years of tenure, the benefit increases to 25 working days.

## Requesting Vacation

- Vacation must be requested at least 15 days in advance.
- The manager must approve the dates considering business continuity.
- Requests are submitted through the HR portal or directly to your manager.

## Carryover Policy

- Unused vacation days can be partially carried over to the following year according to local legislation and internal policies.
- A maximum of 5 days can be carried over to the next calendar year.
- Carried-over days must be used within the first quarter of the new year.

## Peak Periods

- During peak business periods (end of quarter, annual reviews), vacation requests may be limited.
- At least 50% of the team must be available during these periods.

## Emergency Situations

- In case of emergencies, vacation may be cancelled with at least 48 hours notice.
- The company will reimburse any non-refundable expenses incurred.
