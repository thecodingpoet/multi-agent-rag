# Remote Work Policy

## Eligibility

- All employees who have completed their probationary period (90 days) are eligible for remote work.
- Position must be suitable for remote work as determined by the manager.
- Employee must have demonstrated reliable performance and communication.

## Remote Work Options

### Full-Time Remote
- Work from home 5 days per week
- Must attend quarterly in-person team meetings
- Subject to manager approval

### Hybrid Schedule
- Work from home 2-3 days per week
- Core office days (Tuesday-Thursday) recommended for collaboration
- Flexible scheduling based on team needs

### Occasional Remote Work
- Ad-hoc remote work for personal needs or adverse weather
- Requires same-day manager notification

## Requirements

### Home Office Setup
- Dedicated workspace free from distractions
- Reliable high-speed internet connection (minimum 25 Mbps)
- Company-provided laptop and necessary equipment

### Work Hours
- Maintain regular working hours as if in the office
- Be available during core hours (10 AM - 3 PM local time)
- Communicate any schedule changes in advance

### Communication
- Respond to messages within 2 hours during work hours
- Keep calendar updated with availability
- Join all scheduled meetings with camera on when possible

## Equipment and Expenses

### Company-Provided Equipment
- Laptop and necessary software
- Monitor, keyboard, and mouse upon request
- Headset for video calls

### Reimbursable Expenses
- Internet costs: up to $50/month
- Office supplies: up to $25/month
- Ergonomic furniture: one-time stipend of $500

## Security and Confidentiality

- Use only company-approved devices for work
- Connect through VPN when accessing company systems
- Ensure physical security of equipment and documents
- Never share passwords or access credentials

## Performance and Accountability

- Remote work is a privilege that can be revoked if performance declines
- Regular check-ins with manager (at least weekly)
- Participation in all team meetings and activities
