# Employee Onboarding Process

## Before Day One

### HR Preparation (1 week before)
- Send welcome email with start date, time, and location details
- Prepare employee file with signed offer letter and completed forms
- Order equipment (laptop, phone, access cards)
- Create email account and system access credentials

### Manager Preparation
- Set up workspace and equipment
- Prepare 30-60-90 day plan
- Schedule first week meetings and training sessions
- Assign an onboarding buddy

## First Day

### Morning (9:00 AM - 12:00 PM)
- Welcome by HR and office tour
- Complete I-9 verification and remaining paperwork
- Issue equipment and access credentials
- IT setup: laptop, email, VPN, necessary software

### Afternoon (1:00 PM - 5:00 PM)
- Meet with direct manager
- Introduction to team members
- Review role expectations and 90-day goals
- Overview of current projects

## First Week

### Day 2-3: Orientation
- Company history, mission, and values presentation
- Benefits enrollment session
- Security and compliance training
- Introduction to company tools and systems

### Day 4-5: Department Integration
- Shadow team members
- Attend team meetings
- Begin reviewing relevant documentation
- Set up 1-on-1 meetings with key stakeholders

## First Month

### Weeks 2-4
- Complete all mandatory training modules
- Begin contributing to small tasks or projects
- Weekly check-ins with manager and buddy
- Attend department meetings and team events

### End of Month
- 30-day check-in with HR and manager
- Review progress on initial goals
- Address any questions or concerns
- Confirm benefits selections

## 60-Day Check-In

- Formal meeting with manager to review performance
- Assess integration with team and company culture
- Identify any additional training needs
- Adjust goals and expectations as needed

## 90-Day Review

- Comprehensive performance review
- Determine if employee has met probationary period requirements
- Confirm continuation of employment
- Set goals for the next quarter
- Discuss career development opportunities

## Onboarding Buddy Program

### Buddy Responsibilities
- Be the go-to person for questions
- Provide informal guidance and support
- Introduce new hire to colleagues and company culture
- Meet regularly during first 90 days (weekly for first month, bi-weekly thereafter)

### New Hire Responsibilities
- Be proactive in asking questions
- Take initiative in learning and integration
- Provide feedback on onboarding experience
- Build relationships across the organization
