# Laptop Replacement and Upgrade

## Replacement Cycle

- **Standard laptops**: Every 3 years
- **Developer workstations**: Every 2 years
- **Executive devices**: Every 2 years

IT notifies you 60 days before replacement. Track device age at IT portal → "My Equipment"

## Early Replacement Requests

### Eligible Reasons
- Damaged beyond repair
- Performance issues impacting productivity
- Cannot run required software
- Role change

### How to Request
1. Submit ticket at https://helpdesk.company.com → "Hardware" → "Laptop Replacement"
2. Include: Device model/serial, issue description, business impact
3. Get manager approval (for early replacements)
4. IT reviews in 2-3 business days

## Timeline

**Standard**: 12-14 business days
**Expedited** (emergencies): Loaner same/next day, permanent within 7 days

## Data Migration

### Your Responsibility
- Back up files to OneDrive/network drives
- Export browser bookmarks and passwords
- Note installed applications and custom settings

### IT Transfers
- Standard software, email profile, VPN, printers

### Not Transferred
- Local files, browser history, personal apps, temporary files

## Returning Old Device

- Remove personal files and sign out of accounts
- **In office**: Drop at IT help desk
- **Remote**: Ship with pre-paid label
- **Deadline**: 5 business days

**Late fees**: $50/week after 15 days, full device cost after 30 days

## Lost/Damaged Devices

**Report immediately**: ext. 5555

**Replacement cost:**
- 1st incident: Free
- 2nd within 2 years: $200
- 3rd+: Full cost

**Prevention**: Use laptop lock, never leave unattended, enable screen lock after 5 minutes
